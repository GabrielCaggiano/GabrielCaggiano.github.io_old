Color Scheme

The background div behind all the content will be Forest Green (#134611). The Header will be Dark Purple (#321325) with the font being Cultured White (#EFF1ED) in the header and elsewhere. The paragraph titles and subtitles will be Sage(#BCBD8B).

The colors are a based on earth tones, and will try to somewhat mimic nature. It will probably appeal mostly to the outdoor enthusiast.
